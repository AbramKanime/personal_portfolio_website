To-do List App

A utility app for planning your day(s), week(s); by writing down and keeping records and status of tasks planned.

Live Link

Project Overview

The app is a utility tool that the users can use to plan their day(s) and week(s). It gives the users a way to write down the tasks they want to achieve. The app saves the tasks to their local storage. They are also given the options to mark completed tasks, remove any task from the list, and the option to filter between completed and still active tasks. The app also has a feature to delete all tasks at once. The app retains the status of each task between loads; and it is designed to be responsive to different screen sizes.
